Assignment 1: 10/06/16

Challenges:

One of the major challenges I had on this project was in getting the 'X' to sit in the right spot on the ToDoIt notes. I had to run a few google searches to figure out exactly how to do it but I did. It required using two selectors in one CSS function, for want of a better word. My next big issue was that I could not for the life of me figure out how to get the footer to stick to the bottom. It turns out I had it all right except that I needed to set the position of the 'html' selector to relative and the min-height to 100%.

What did I learn/already know?

In the course of completing this project, I learned CSS from the ground up. I knew nothing before Thursday the 29th of September when I sat down and read those tutorials. This was supplemented, of course, by the information given in class. My biggest takeaway from the assignment is a good one: I very capable of, and even relish, teaching myself how to program using the resources available to me. I am sure this will be an excellent quality going forward.

Resources:

I basically taught myself CSS on the first day after this assignment was posted using primarily W3Schools, a online tutorial site. Also, I must credit Mr. Albert Morgan with revealing the final critical piece to the footer puzzle.
